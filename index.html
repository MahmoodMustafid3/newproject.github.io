Courses We Offer

We offers yearly courses and six months courses

Diploma in Fire and
Safety Engineering

The Diploma in Fire and Safety Engineering is a primary level fire and safety course, its duration is 1 year. Students will get access to the facilities and the best equipment's and Technology. The faculty and mentors are skilled professionals.

Diploma in Industrial
Safety Management

Diploma in Industrial Safety Management (1 year) is a program intiated by the Govt. of India for employees and students who are in the field of Occupational risks and health issues. Aimed at empowering them with knowledge.

Diploma in Environment
Health Safety

Diploma in Environmental health and Safety Engineering is a education programme covering comprehensive Safety Management concerning industries which are dealing with hazardous materials and chemicals that can cause harm to Environment and employees health.

Fire Officer

Diploma in Fire Officer is especially designed for fire and safety officers for getting certified in the Management of fire and safety Engineering

Safety Officer

A safety officer training will teach the course delegates to understand workplace safety laws and legislations. How to conduct risk assessments and implement control measures to prevent accidents and injuries from happening. The position of Safety Officer is an important role within any organization.

Bsc Fire

B.Sc Fire & Safety or Bachelor of Science in Fire & Safety is an Under-Graduate Management course. Fire and Safety studies simulated firefighting exercise as well as applied physics, chemistry, mathematics.
